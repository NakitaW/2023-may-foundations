ABOUT ME

You might not be familiar with me or my work, but you soon will. Hello new friend! My name is Nakita and I'm happy to have you along the journey with me as I perfect my love for photography. I currently reside in Omaha, Nebraska and I consider myself a woman of many talents including writing, videography and photography. I have a bachelors in communications and a masters in marketing.
The best way I can describe myself is a "creative in a rut" and I'm currently in the process of breaking that spell. Not only is it my outlet, but it has also given me the space to strengthen my love for photography and will hopefully be a useful medium to catapult me into the industry of freelance photography among other media. I appreciate anyone who plans to read and join me along for the journey.
